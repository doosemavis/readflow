For the way you actually read

One reader,
countless ways
of seeing.

Most reading apps were designed for one kind of eye. TailorMyText starts from the opposite premise: every reader brings a different visual system, attention shape, and energy budget — and the page should bend to meet them, not the other way round.

i.
Dyslexia & reading fatigue

Wider letter spacing, tuned fonts, and reading-guide overlays that calm the line you're on without freezing the rest of the page.

Reading guide · spacing · fonts
ii.
Low vision & aging eyes

Step text up to magazine-poster scale without losing the document's structure. High-contrast and warm-paper themes, side by side.

Scale · contrast · sepia
iii.
ADHD & sensory overload

A single line in focus, the rest gently dimmed. Section navigation that lets you skip without losing where you were.

Focus mode · sectioning
iv.
Eye strain & long sessions

Night theme that stays warm enough to read by, line heights that let the page breathe, and zero animation noise once you're reading.

Night mode · airy spacing
v.
Color sensitivity

Six accessibility-oriented font families, four palettes, and tunable intensity — combine until the page genuinely disappears into the words.

Palettes · intensity
vi.
Privacy as accessibility

Documents you upload are auto-deleted seven days after you last opened them. Storage is per-account; nothing is shared, nothing trains a model.

7-day TTL · private bucket
